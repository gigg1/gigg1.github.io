复杂网络中寻找多个传播节点的两种基准方法：

method advantage disadvantage
chooses the top k influential nodes as spreaders according to a centrality index simple most of these k spreaders may be clustered in a community.
chooses k unconnected spreaders according to a centrality index many spreaders may still locate in a community.

Methods:

  1. 从整个网络中选取super node的步骤（Blondel method）：

  2. each community adjusts their nodes according to the increment of modularity

  3. each community is replaced by a new node called “super node”

  4. The two steps are repeated until the modularity can not be improved
    （to obtain a great many communities, the two steps are iterated only once）

  5. 红黑树：

所有的super nodes存储在一棵红黑树中，一个super node的key是节点的编号，values包含它的大小（包含的节点数目）、其中的节点和指示节点是否被访问过的状态变量。

  1. 选取重要节点群的步骤：

  2. 首先从红黑树中选取最大的super node。

  3. 然后从这个super node中根据一种中心性指标选取最有影响力的节点。
  4. 以相同方法从红黑树中选取下一个super node，然后从这个super node中选取最有影响力的节点，这个最有影响力的节点不能和已经选取过最有影响力节点的super node有边相连。
  5. 如果所有super node都已经访问过并且选出来的传播节点的数量不够，则根据大小降序访问所有super node并且选取剩余传播节点。
  6. 重复以上步骤直到k个传播节点都被选取。

（实际上，super node的数量远大于k，k个传播节点往往在第一遍搜索时都可以被找到）

测试二

method explanation time complexity
Super-node O(m+n+(k+l)logl),m is the number of edges in network.n is the number of nodes in network.l is the number of super nodes.k is the number of the speakers.
K-medoid Chooses k spreaders from communities.Each edge(u, v) is randomly designated either “open” with probability \beta_{uv} or “closed” with probability 1-\beta_{uv} independently. The \beta_{uv} is defined as \beta_{uv}=1-(1-\beta)^{w_{uv}},w_{uv} is the weight of edge(u,v) and \beta is a designated propagation probability.For two nodes p and q,if there is at least a path between them which is composed of “open” edges, ω(p,q) = 1, otherwise 0.Then the element m_{pq}of information transfer probability matrix M is defined as m_{pq}=\frac{1}{N}\sum^N_{i=1}\omega(p,q),N is the number of sampling. The network is first divided into k communities based on M and then k medoids are chosen as k spreaders. O(k(n-k)^2), n is the number of nodes in network.k is the number of the speakers.
